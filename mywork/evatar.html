Overview

Evatar is an agentic AI platform that automates your entire social media presence. At the center is Eva — a voice-to-content creative assistant that turns raw thoughts into ready-to-post content. Avatars generate videos, and agent workflows handle posting, scheduling and engagement across platforms.

I built Evatar from idea to live beta in 16 weeks and now lead product, engineering, creative and go-to-market. The platform is in active beta with paying users and running case studies with founders, creators and operators.

Product Demo

The Problem

Social media is essential but exhausting for founders and small teams.

  • Constant content ideation and planning
  • Platform-specific formatting and optimization
  • Consistent posting across multiple channels
  • Keeping a coherent voice while staying authentic
  • Ongoing replies to comments and DMs

Most people either neglect social entirely or burn out trying to keep up. Existing tools either only schedule posts or become yet another system that needs managing.

Core Product

🎙️ Eva — Creative Assistant

  • Turns voice notes into scripts, posts and video narratives
  • Expands rough ideas into structured content in your tone
  • Rewrites, polishes and repurposes content across formats
  • Guides you through what to post and why

🎬 AI Video Avatars

  • Unlimited avatars from a single image via Argil
  • Realistic lip-sync driven by ATOM model
  • Multiple visual styles and templates
  • Voice cloning through ElevenLabs

🤖 Autonomous Posting

  • Multi-platform posting to IG, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn
  • Scheduling with platform-aware formatting
  • Caption and hashtag generation
  • Auto-replies to comments and DMs

⚡ Multi-Model Layer

  • ATOM for avatar rendering and motion
  • Sora-style B-roll generation (beta)
  • OpenAI & Perplexity for reasoning
  • Planned Veo integration

What I Learned

  • Consistency beats perfect content every time
  • Voice UX alone isn't enough — people need agent flows that do the work
  • Most users don't want "a tool", they want finished output
  • Realistic avatars only matter when tied to clear content strategy
  • Speed of iteration beats long planning cycles
  • Performance metrics make content systems feel "intelligent" instead of random

What's Next

  • Full mobile app with voice-first Eva assistant
  • Deeper automated replies and community engagement
  • Self-learning content loops that adjust based on performance
  • New formats: text posts with visuals, blog posts, avatar-free videos
  • AI strategy and insights dashboard
  • API for agencies and multi-brand teams
